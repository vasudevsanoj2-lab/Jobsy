About Jobsy

Jobsy is a digital platform designed to connect daily wage workers with users who require reliable services in a transparent and efficient way. The admin panel allows centralized control over worker verification, complaint management, and service quality.

By keeping the interface clean and distraction-free, Jobsy enables administrators to focus on trust, accountability, and smooth platform operations.
