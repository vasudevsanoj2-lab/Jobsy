About Jobsy

Jobsy is a digital platform built to support daily wage workers. Workers can apply for open jobs, communicate with employers, track job progress, manage work history, and access genuine opportunities to improve their daily income.

Our mission is to create a transparent and easy job-connection system for job seekers and employers.
